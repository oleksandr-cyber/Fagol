Best pets in grow a garden

For new players the best pet is the dog. The dog digs up a random seed, but there is one problem: it has only a 5% chance of digging up a seed.

T-rex

Every 25 minutes, it bites 4 random fruits and gives a random mutation.

Kitsune

Every 22 minutes, it goes to another garden, mutates to Chakra and makes a duplicate.

Dragonfly

Every 5 minutes, one random crop will turn gold.

Red Fox

Every 6 minutes, it tries to duplicate a seed from another player (it can be a candy blossom).

Racoon

Every 15 minutes, it goes to another garden and makes a duplicate.

Queen Bee

1. Every 20 minutes, a fruit becomes a Pollinated mutation.

2. Every 23 minutes, the pet with the highest cooldown refreshes its abilities.

Seal

When you sell your pet, it has a 2.42% chance to get the pet back as its egg equivalent.

Koi

This pet is in the Zen Shop.

It grants a chance to recover an egg when hatching.

Mimic Octopus

Every 20 minutes, it mimics and copies an ability from a random pet!

Disco Bee

Every 15 minutes, it has a 16% chance that a nearby fruit becomes a Disco mutation.

Owl

It boosts all active pets, which will gain around 0.20 XP each second.

Night Owl

All active pets gain an additional 0.28 XP per second.

Blood Owl

All active pets gain 0.50 XP per second.

Cooked Owl

1. Every 15 minutes, it has a 15.35% chance to cook a nearby fruit.

2. All active pets gain an additional 0.17 XP/s.

Corrupted Kitsune

Every 20:53m, it launches cursed energy at 9 different fruits. Each fruit has a 20.35% chance to mutate with Corrupt Chakra, with a very rare chance for Corrupt Foxfire Chakra instead.

Chicken Zombie

1. Every 25 minutes, it has a 20% chance that a nearby fruit becomes Zombified.

2. It increases egg hatch speed by 10%.

Bald Eagle

Wings of Freedom: Every ~7m, it takes flight and spreads its wings. All eggs advance their hatch time by -70s! There is a ~70% chance for the time advanced to be multiplied by ~2x.

Blood Kiwi

Every 60 seconds, it reduces the hatch time of the egg with the highest remaining hatch time by 45 seconds and increases its hatch speed by 20%.

Echo Frog

Every 5 minutes, it croaks and causes a random nearby plant to advance growth by 24 hours.

Mole

Every 80 seconds, it digs into the ground and finds Sheckles or Gears.

Parasaurolophus

Occasionally, it goes to the cosmetic crate with the highest time and reduces the time to open.

Golden Bee

The Golden Bee has the ability to occasionally pollinate fruit, and there is a small chance that harvested fruit will turn gold.

Spinosaurus

Every 20 minutes, it devours a random mutation from 3 fruits in the user's garden, then roars, spreading those mutations to a random fruit in the garden! It prioritizes favorited fruits.

Fennec Fox

Every 22:12m, the Fennec Fox goes to a random fruit in a player's garden, copies one mutation from it, then applies it to any fruit in the pet owner's garden. The higher a mutation's multiplier is, the rarer it will be for the pet to succeed in copying said mutation. It cannot put mutations on favorited plants.

Axolotl

It has a 6.76% chance for a Summer type fruit to stay after collecting.

Hyacinth Macaw

Every 8 minutes, it has a 15.63% chance to mutate a nearby fruit, applying the Cloudtouched mutation.

Scarlet Macaw

Every 8 minutes, it has a 16.00% chance to mutate a nearby fruit (chance can vary by age), applying the Verdant mutation.

Moon Cat

Every 70 seconds, it naps for 20 seconds. New fruit within 20 studs will be 1.5x larger. It has a 6% chance for Night Type fruits to stay after harvest.

Silver Monkey

The Silver Monkey gives an 8% chance to refund crops back to a player's inventory. Rarer crops have a lower chance of receiving these refunds.

Griffin

Every 15 minutes, it flaunts its wings and releases a Cyclone. Pets struck have their cooldown advanced by 66 seconds, and fruits struck have a 10% chance to get the Cyclonic mutation.

Phoenix

1. It goes to 4 random fruits to apply the Flaming mutation; fruits passed along the way have a ~20% chance to get the Flaming mutation.

Pets taken from the mutation machine have a bonus to their age.
